    • ABOUT TENEBRAS

    TENEBRAS LIGHTING & ELECTRICAL TRADING is a manufacturer, fabricator, and distributor of electrical, industrial, and high voltage transformerss since 2013. We manufacture a wide range of transformers such as pole-mounted, dry-type, and pad-mounted, manufactured according to NEMA standards. It is our aim to provide economical power equipment with advanced winding design features for reducing no load and load losses. For Electrical Distribution, we supply industrial lighting and electrical wires. We also fabricate Panel Board Enclosures, Unitized Panel of low and medium voltage, Switch Gears for Automatic and Manuel Transfer Switches, Cable Trays, Wire Ways, Cable Ladders, Busbar Gutter and Breaker Gutters, Automatic Capacitor Tank, Motor Control Center, and Customized Panel Boards. Our company dedicates itself to meet client requirements also through assuming the role of technical consultant according to custom specifications and budgetary considerations.
